tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 500g ground beef or chicken
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • Lettuce, shredded
  • Cheese, grated
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Cook meat with spices and chopped onions until browned.
  2. Warm tortillas on a skillet.
  3. Fill each tortilla with meat, then top with vegetables, cheese, and sauces.
  4. Serve immediately while warm.

pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various other ingredients, baked at a high temperature.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Tomatoes
  • Olives
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Mix warm water, yeast, and a pinch of sugar. Let sit for 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to form dough. Let rise 1 hour.
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  5. Spread tomato sauce, add cheese and toppings.
  6. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden.

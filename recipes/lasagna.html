lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food made by layering sheets of pasta with a savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and lots of cheese.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  4. Add ground beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned.
  5. Pour in tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 30–40 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
